  1. Turn on the phone: Power on by pressing and holding the power button.
  2. Wi-Fi & Mobile Data: Connect to Wi-Fi or activate mobile data if you will be using that for internet access.
  3. Sign in to Google Account: Make sure to sign in (if necessary) for access to the Google Play Store, Gmail, and other Google services.
  4. Basic Navigation: Show how to use the home screen, open apps, and use the Notification Panel (swipe down from the top).
  5. Making Calls & Texts: Guide to make a call, send a text, and use the Phone and Messages apps.
  6. Setting up Shortcuts: Help set up shortcuts on the home screen for easy access to apps like YouTube, Gmail, etc.

Phone Features:

  • Gestures (up, down, left/right) for navigation.
  • Camera for taking pictures and videos.
  • Google Assistant for voice commands, if desired.

During Initial Setup:

  • Insert a SIM Card if we have it:
    • Before turning on the phone, insert your SIM card into the SIM slot.
    • If AT&T supports eSIM, you can activate it during the initial setup or later through the device settings.
  • Mobile Data Activation:
    • During setup, the phone will ask if you want to enable Mobile Data.
    • You can turn it on by confirming when prompted.
  • If No SIM is Installed: Without a SIM card, the phone wonâ€™t connect to a mobile network for data, and it will default to Wi-Fi only.

Steps to Enable Mobile Data After Setup:

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Go to Network & internet.
  3. Tap SIMs (or Mobile network, depending on your Android version).
  4. Toggle Mobile data to On.

Turning on your Motorola Razr Plus:

Press and hold the Power button for a few seconds (the long skinny button on the lower right).

Wait for the Motorola logo or boot animation to appear on the screen and release the button.

After boot-up, check for symbols at the bottom of the screen, such as a gear wheel, red/yellow/blue circle, and a phone icon.

Setup Phone for Data, Not Wi-Fi:

Accessing Data and Wi-Fi Settings

  • Swipe down twice from the top of the screen to access the Quick Settings Panel.
  • Tap the Settings gear icon in the bottom-right corner.

Navigate to Wi-Fi Settings:

  • In the Settings menu, select Network & internet.
  • Tap Wi-Fi to view and manage Wi-Fi networks.

Navigate to Mobile Data Settings:

  • Tap SIMs (or Mobile network) and toggle Mobile data to On.

Contacts and Making Calls:

Adding a New Contact:

  1. Open the Contacts app and tap the "+" button.
  2. Enter Contact Details: Name, Phone number, Email, and other details.
  3. Tap "Save" when done.

Saving a Contact from a Call or Text:

  1. Open the Phone or Messages app and tap the contact info.
  2. Tap "Add to Contacts", enter the details, and tap "Save".

Turn Off Phone:

  1. Press the Power and Volume Up buttons at the same time.
  2. Press "Turn Off" on the screen that appears.

Yes, You Can Access YouTube Without an Account:

  1. Open the Google app or Chrome browser and search for "YouTube".
  2. You can browse and watch videos, but you need to sign in for liking, commenting, or subscribing.

Placing a YouTube Shortcut on Your Home Screen:

  • Open YouTube in Chrome, tap the menu icon, and select "Add to Home screen".
  • Using Google App, search for YouTube and follow the same process.

What Happens After Adding the Shortcut:

A YouTube icon will appear on your home screen, allowing direct access to YouTube.

Swipe Gestures on the Motorola Razr (2024):

  • Swipe Up: Access your App Drawer or recent apps.
  • Swipe Down: Open the notification panel and quick settings.
  • Swipe Left/Right: Switch between home screens or app tabs.
